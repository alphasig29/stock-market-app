Welcome to the Stonk Pit


Once you login, you will have access to monitor the market sectors in real-time, as well as creating your own list of stocks to follow.

Enjoy your time on The Stonk Pit!

Existing Key Features:
  • Register and Log In to an account, using APIs.
  • Navigation is locked down by Authentication and User's Roles
  • Home landing page has Random Quote, Login/Register feature, Site Title and Footer sections.
  • Stock Market Sector route that retreives live S&P 500 data on a 5 second interval, and uses color & ordering to reflect performance.
  • Authorization / Role Guards have been placed on all Routes.
  • Loggin out re-routes to the home page for the user to log in again.
  • UI Stubs exist for the Stock Watch List and Administration features.
